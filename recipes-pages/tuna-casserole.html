TUNA CASSEROLE

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups cooked macaroni
  • 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 Â½ cups French fried onions

STEPS

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine cooked macaroni, tuna, and condensed soup together in a 9x13-inch baking dish; sprinkle with cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly, about 25 minutes. Sprinkle with fried onions; bake 5 minutes more.
